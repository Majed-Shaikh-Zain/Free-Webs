Using the following information, you will be able to determine the significance of each color-coded slot. Please pay close attention, as this explanation will be your only guidance for interpreting the schedule.


If you find a Red button, it signifies that the corresponding timeline is occupied by an external, uncontrollable factor, such as a university lecture or an important meeting. These slots cannot be managed or altered manually.


If you encounter an Orange button, it indicates that the timeline is currently in use and reflects an activity happening at this moment.


Finally, a Green button represents an available or flexible task. This could denote a task that has already been completed or one that remains to be accomplished.


Additionally, consider implementing a method to track the status of previously completed sessions. For example, you might introduce a fourth color to identify finished tasks or adopt another innovative approach to visually distinguish between completed and pending sessions.
